## Back-to-Back Opportunities

### March: Italian Swing
- Mar 2-4: Misano (fly LDN→BLQ)

- Mar 12-14: NLS 1 Nürburgring (drive MXP→Nürb, 7h or fly)
- Mar 15: Imola (fly CGN→BLQ)
- Mar 19-22: NLS 2 + Nurb Test (drive from London makes sense)
- Mar 23-25: Spa Test (Nurb→Spa 1.5h drive)

**Optimization:** Stay in Italy Mar 2-11, then base in Germany Mar 12-25
